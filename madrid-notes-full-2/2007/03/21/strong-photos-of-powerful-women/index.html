Strong photos of Powerful women…

21 March, 2007 (16:31) | Art in Madrid

…well that isn’t the title of the exhibition at EFTI but it might as well be! If you like the idea of artistic shots of some of the most famous ladies from the last 50 years of film and fashion then pop down to this end of Madrid (it’s a five minute walk from Menedez Pelayo Metro station at Calle Fuenterrabía, 4-6 - map). The exhibition runs until April 17th - half an hour in the company of these women is invogorating, inspiring stuff!

Question, where else can you see good photography exhibits in Madrid?
