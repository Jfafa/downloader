]]> Members Meeting – Wednesday 3rd October 2018 http://www.sadla.org.uk/index.php/archives/815 Wed, 05 Dec 2018 17:24:50 +0000 http://www.sadla.org.uk/?p=815 Continue reading "Members Meeting – Wednesday 3rd October 2018"]]> An update on Universal Credit by the DWP. 
The universal credit will be introduced in Sheffield from November 2018, and will bring six benefits under one organisation:
  • Housing Benefit
  • Income-related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA)
  • Child Tax Credit
  • Working Tax Credit
  • Income Support

Jenifer Marsden from DWP explained the implications to Landlords and the alternative payments available.

Universal credit  will provide the following benefits:

  • one benefit, one place to claim
  • one benefit that steps with you in and out of work simple rates for limited capability
  • admin under DWP
  • claim online
  • Claimants see all their info
  • change circumstances online
  • is based on everyone in the household

 

]]> AGM and Members Meeting Thursday 7th June, 2018 http://www.sadla.org.uk/index.php/archives/807 Fri, 08 Jun 2018 11:26:09 +0000 http://www.sadla.org.uk/?p=807 Continue reading "AGM and Members Meeting Thursday 7th June, 2018"]]> Michelle Houston (Sheffield City Council) gave an update on both National issues and Local issues.

Her presentation can be found here SADLA update 7 June 2018.

Some key points of her presentation are:
Key changes in the Housing and planning Act 2016, including the introduction of Civil Penalties and Banning orders, the introduction of a Rogue Landlords register (for local authorities only),  Rent repayment orders.
HMO’s will include dwellings of 5 bedrooms or more on any number of floors. This is likely to add 600-1000 extra licenses to the Sheffield HMO’s.
‘Fit for Human Habitation’ private members bill, allowing tenants to take their landlords to court were landlords fail on category 1 hazards.
