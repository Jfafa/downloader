The annual membership fee for 2002/03 is £70. Membership of SADLA includes free membership of the NFRL, and you will receive their quarterly colour magazine, keeping you informed of new legislation and nationally organised events relevant to private sector landlords.

SADLA offers its members regular seminars covering a wide variety of landlord specific topics, and gives an opportunity for members to network. The SADLA membership card gives access to discounts on insurance, finance, building supplies, furnishings and property maintenance.
